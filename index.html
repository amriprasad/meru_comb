Meru Combinatorics 2023
ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

This is the first of a series of annual conferences on combinatorics in India: the Meru Annual Combinatorics Conference.

Meru stands for the sacred mountain of ancient Indian cosmology, and also for the triangle of binomial coefficients studied by Indian prosodists.

Dates: 28-05-2023 to 01-06-2023

Venue: Pondicherry University
